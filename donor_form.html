Be a plasma donor and save a life

People who have fully recovered from COVID-19 have antibodies in their plasma that can attack the virus. This convalescent plasma is being evaluated as treatment for patients with serious or immediately life-threatening COVID-19 infections or those judged by a healthcare provider to be at high risk of progression to severe or life-threatening disease .If more people start to donate, we can save more lives on a daily basis. Then those recovered patients can donate, which will help us in keeping this cycle running and saving more lives. We need survivors like you.

DONOR ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA: Donors must have had a prior infection of COVID-19 and must have recovered from symptoms at least 14 days prior to donation. In addition they should have had symptoms like breathing problem, cough, cold, fever, body ache at the time of Covid. The stronger the symptoms, the stronger the antibodies Not have diabetes, Not have a pregnancy, Not have high blood pressure, should be above 18 and not be over the age of 65.

NOTE: THIS IS INFORMATION COLLATED FROM PUBLICLY AVAILABLE WEBSITES. WE ARE NOT, NOR DO WE CLAIM TO BE, PROPONENTS NOR ANY AUTHORITY ON THIS PROCEDURE. PLEASE CONSULT YOUR DOCTOR FOR COMPLETE DETAILS.
