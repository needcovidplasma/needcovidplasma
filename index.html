Our Mission

NeedCovidPlasma.in is an individual organization and a platform intended to connect donors,recepients and volunteers

The country has seen large scale devastation as an effect of COVID-19.Plasma from those who have recovered could be a key part of the fight against COVID-19.
We know how terrifying it is to run from pillar to post to find the right blood donor, in the absence of an obvious place or institution to reach out to.
NeedCovidplasma.in has been created to make sure this crucial time isnâ€™t wasted in looking in the wrong place. In a situation where time is of great essence,
NeedCovidplasma.in is a small initiative towards solving the problem of finding eligible donors.
We at "NeedCovidplasma.in" aspire to connect you with eligible donors.
We built this platform to serve anyone in need, and hence do not support any monetization of plasma donation.
We sincerely hope that this bridge which we have named "NeedCovidplasma" will make an impact and help saving lives of your loved ones.

Our Policy

We "NeedCovidplasma.in" are just an online platform aimed at connecting volunteers,plasma donors and recipients together.
We are not into any medical services such as testing,plasma transfusion or etc.
We neither engage ourselves into any monetary benifits nor do we promote any monetary transaction among the volunteers, donors and recipients.
We just act as a bridge and are not liable to any discrepency in the information provided by the donors or the patients.
